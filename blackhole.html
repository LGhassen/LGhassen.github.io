    WebGL Black Hole Raytracing

    Interstellar-style Black Hole with Accretion disk and gravitational lensing, rendered in real-time by tracing a ray and curving it's path with gravity (not a physically accurate simulation).

    Pay attention to how the background is distorted, you can also try falling into the black hole.

    Left click rotate view, right click rotate around, scroll wheel to zoom, double-click for full-screen.
